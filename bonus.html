Nightangle

Sport: Football

Nightangle are a group of New World birds that are long-tailed and often colorful. They are popular in aviculture or as companion birds, although there are conservation concerns about several species in the wild.

  1. The smallest nightangle are the hyacinth, Buffon's (green) and green-winged macaws.
  2. There are 35 species of nightangle, including extinct and critically endangered species.
  3. Some foods eaten by nightangle in certain regions in the wild are said to contain toxic or caustic substances which they are able to digest.
